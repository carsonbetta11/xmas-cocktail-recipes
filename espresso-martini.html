Espresso Martini

Ingredients

  • 2oz vodka
  • 1oz Kahlúa
  • 0.5oz simple syrup
  • 1 ‘shot’ of espresso
  • 3 coffee beans

Instructions

  1. This is the most complex cocktail in my collection! Don’t be intimidated.
  2. First, pull a single shot of espresso into a random cup using the espresso machine.
  3. Make sure you grind the coffee somewhere in the ‘fine’ range on our coffee grinder, and only grind as much as you need.
  4. Do not use our regular grind of coffee.
  5. As the espresso shot is pouring, add vodka, Kahlúa, and simple syrup into a cocktail shaker with ice.
  6. Add in the espresso when it’s ready, and shake for 10 seconds.
  7. Strain into a LARGE martini or margarita glass.
  8. Add 3 coffee beans as a garnish on the surface.
