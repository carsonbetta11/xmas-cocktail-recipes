Gimlet

Ingredients

  • 2.5oz gin (or vodka)
  • 0.5oz lime juice
  • 0.5oz simple syrup
  • 1 lime wedge or slice

Instructions

  1. Add gin, lime juice, and simple syrup into a shaker.
  2. Shake resolutely for 10–12 seconds, then strain into a martini glass.
  3. Garnish with lime.
