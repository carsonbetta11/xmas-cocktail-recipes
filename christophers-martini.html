Christopher’s Martini

Ingredients

  • 2.5oz gin
  • 0.75oz dry vermouth
  • 1 lemon twist

Instructions

  1. Cut a lemon peel twist, rub it along the edge of a martini glass, then drop it inside.
  2. Pour gin and vermouth into a mixing glass with ice; stir for 10 seconds, then strain into the glass.
