Margarita

Ingredients

  • 2oz tequila blanco (clear)
  • 0.5oz Cointreau or Grand Marnier
  • 1oz lime juice
  • 0.5oz agave syrup
  • 1 lime wedge

Instructions

  1. Fill a rocks glass with ice and add the lime wedge, first rubbing the lime around the lip of the glass.
  2. Add the tequila, Cointreau, lime juice, and agave syrup into a shaker and shake for 10–12 seconds.
  3. Strain into the rocks glass and serve.
