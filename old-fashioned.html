Old Fashioned

Ingredients

  • 2oz bourbon
  • 2 tsp water
  • 1 tsp sugar or simple syrup
  • 1 round ice cube
  • 1 orange slice
  • 1 cherry

Instructions

  1. In a short glass, add the round ice cube, water, and sugar.
  2. Next, slowly pour the bourbon over the ice cube, and spin the ice cube a few times to help water down the mixture.
  3. Garnish with the orange slice and cherry.
