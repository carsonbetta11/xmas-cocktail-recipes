Negroni

Ingredients

  • 1oz gin
  • 1oz Campari
  • 1oz sweet vermouth
  • 1 orange slice

Instructions

  1. Add ice to a rocks glass, then pour in gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth.
  2. Rub an orange slice around the lip of the glass, then add it into the glass and stir for several seconds, then serve.
